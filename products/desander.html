Product description

Desander is solids control equipment with a set of desander cyclone to separate sand from drilling mud in drilling rigs&peroid; Desander is installed on top of the mud tank following the shale shaker and vacuum degasser but before desilter&peroid; The desander removes the abrasive solids around 45-74 micron&peroid;

Desander Equipment In Solids Control System

A desander is the second phase cleaning for a whole solids control system, It sit on mud tank for separate drilling cuttings 47-76 micron and above by centrifugal force. The untreated mud will feed into desander cone at the horizontal inlet at pressure, the pressure and weight will help solids particles moving beside cone wall and drop down, fluids particles will be press on top and over flow away through pipeline. There can be a shaker to collect solids particles from desander nozzle or drop onto a hoper directly.
